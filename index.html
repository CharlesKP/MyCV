About Me

Hello there! My name is Charles. I am a Musician and more recently a Web Developer.

I have been playing music for almost 30 years now. Taking the piano from age eight and later picked up the guitar, taking music as a subject throughout my school career. I studied classical Music at the University of Cape Town and I have performed all over South Africa and abroad over the years over the past 18 years being a professional musician. I also run a home studio.

While at high school, I took Computer Science till Grade 12. We mainly learned how to program in Turbo Pascal (similar to C# and Delphi) during those days. Since then I have done brief online courses in Javascript and Python. I am currently enrolled in a Fullstack Webdevelopment Bootcamp with HyperionDev. So far we have focussed on Javascript, HTML, CSS and SCSS as well as well as various other elements of software/web development.

On a more personal note. I love spending time with friends and family. I am a collaborative person and am interested in how the mind works.
